Karkoon — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What Karkoon does

Karkoon is a voice-first accounting app. You speak, type or photograph transactions; Karkoon converts them into double-entry vouchers and reports.

Data we collect

Your accounting entries (ledgers, vouchers, party names, amounts) are stored on your device only. We do not run servers that receive or store your books. Backups you download stay with you.

Microphone

The microphone is used only when you tap the mic or enable the wake word, solely to convert your speech into a transaction. Audio is processed by your device's / browser's speech service and is not stored by Karkoon.

Camera

The camera is used only when you tap Scan, to read an invoice/receipt you choose. If AI document scanning is enabled, the photo is sent to the AI provider (Anthropic) solely to extract the transaction details, then discarded per their data policy. Photos are not stored by Karkoon.

What we never do

No ads, no sale of data, no analytics profiles of your finances, no background recording.

Your control

Delete the app (or clear its data) and your books are gone from the device. Use Settings → Backup to keep your own copy.

Contact

Questions: contact the developer via the app's store listing.
